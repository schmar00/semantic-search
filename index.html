This example is built to search the EGDI (Micka) CSW catalogue web service. For the search logic is used the new GeoERA Keyword Thesaurus hosted at the Geological Survey of Austria (Sparql endpoint).

To perform a semantic search via keywords
- select a keyword promoted in the dropdown list or press Enter to select the upper most. The search passes through following steps trying to find a maximum of 20 results:

  1. search term in English in keywords
  2. search term in English in title texts
  3. matches of keywords within a semantic distance (related and all narrower)
  4. text matches in title texts within a semantic distance (related and all narrower)
  5. any text matches over all of the search term (incl. parts of texts)
  6. matches of keywords of broader terms
  7. text matches in title of broader terms
Relevance criteria for sorting the results list are calculated as follows:

10 pts for the search term as keyword / 3 pts each for narrower or related keyword matches / 1 pt for each broader match / 3 pts for the search term in title / 2 pts for each occurence of the search term in abstract / and finally 1 pt for partly text matches in keywords. The total sum of points is cut by 12 pts and converted to percents.
Note:
Keywords are searched against catalogue texts in English language. But the language of the keywords offered to search is choosen by the browser language. About 30% terms are available in 23 non-english languages.
If a term typed in and searched by Go! button - a full text search (language dependent!) is carried out.
